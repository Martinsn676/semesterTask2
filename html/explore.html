For you between 8 and 16

Are you a young person looking to learn more about science? Come on down to our museum, there’s plenty to see and do.

You can learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth? Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to learn and explore..

We also have the Young Stars club which meets once a week on a Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00 where you’ll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts..

During the school holidays we run special holiday clubs where you can join other children your age to go on a journey of discovery. Each holiday we pick a new theme to explore. To find out more about the holiday club and how you can join, send us a message.

For young families

Welcome to our science museum! We understand the importance of fostering a love for science in young minds, and we're thrilled to offer a range of engaging activities for your young family.

One of the highlights of our museum is the Young Explorers Club, a special program designed to ignite your child's curiosity and nurture their passion for scientific exploration. As a member of the club, your child will have access to exclusive events and hands-on activities guided by our team of expert educators. They'll have the opportunity to conduct exciting experiments, engage in interactive demonstrations, and participate in captivating workshops tailored to their age group.

The Young Explorers Club meets regularly throughout the year, providing a consistent and enriching experience for your child. Our dedicated staff members are committed to creating a safe and supportive environment where young scientists can thrive. By joining the club, your child will not only develop a deeper understanding of scientific concepts but also enhance their critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork skills.

In addition to the club, our museum offers a wide range of exhibits that cater specifically to young learners. From interactive displays and immersive simulations to captivating shows and live demonstrations, we strive to make science accessible and exciting for children of all ages. Our exhibits are designed to inspire wonder, encourage exploration, and ignite a lifelong passion for learning.

We also organize special events and workshops for parents, providing you with resources and insights to support your child's scientific development. Our knowledgeable staff members are always available to answer your questions, offer guidance, and share educational tips that you can incorporate into your family's everyday life.

By visiting our museum and becoming part of the Young Explorers Club, you're investing in your child's future and setting them on a path of discovery and innovation. We can't wait to embark on this scientific journey together with your young family!

For teachers

As a community-driven museum, we want to work with schools to create places of learning and exploring. Our team are on hand to give your students guided tours of the museum, teach them in our learning laboratory, and provide great video presentations that will excite and inspire them.

Please get in touch with us here to find out more about our facilities and to arrange a time to bring your students through for a visit.

For tesearchers

Are you looking to get involved with our team of researchers and academics? Our museum offers various ways for you to use our resources and contribute towards them. We have online records, laboratory space, and a working relationship with a number of universities around the country.
